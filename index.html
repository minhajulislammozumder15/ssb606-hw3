BANGLADESH IS A DEVELOPING COUNTRY


Bangladesh is a riverine country. According to Bangladesh Water development board (BWDB)[1] about 230 rivers currently flow in Bangladesh (during summer and winter), although the numbers stated in some sources are ambiguous.[2][3][4] As stated by a publication called বাংলাদেশের নদ-নদী by BWDB (Bangladesh Water development board), 310 rivers flow in the summer although they republished another study in 6 volumes where stated 405 rivers.
